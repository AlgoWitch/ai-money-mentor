## The Vision: Actionable Wealth, Not Just Data

In India, **92% of the workforce** in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities lacks access to personalized, affordable financial advice. Traditional apps are static ledgers—they show you where your money *went*, but they don't tell you where it *should go*. The result is a persistent **"Action Gap"**—users have the intent to save and invest but lack the confidence to execute.

**Niveshak AI (निवेशक AI)** is the answer. We have built an **Agentic Financial Control Plane** that transforms raw transactional data into a dynamic, personalized **FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) Roadmap**.

Niveshak AI is not an app; it is a **Sovereign Wealth Mentor** that uses safe, localized, and culturally empathetic AI to help every Indian build a robust **"Suraksha Kawach"** (Emergency Fund) and a secure retirement corpus.

### **Our "Expert-Level" Pillars:**

1.  **Local Privacy Shield (SDM):**
    A client-side **Sensitive Data Masking** layer that redacts all Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as account numbers and names, *before* the data is transmitted to the cloud.

2.  **Agentic Orchestration (Groq / Llama 3.1):**
    Unlike simple chatbots, our backend features a dynamic **Reasoning Loop** managed by a specialized **Agentic Controller**. It coordinates three distinct agents:
    * **The Parser Agent:** Extracts JSON structures from unstructured Bank SMS, Form 16s, and PDF statements with near-100% accuracy.
    * **The FIRE Planner Agent:** A robust, math-driven simulator that calculates Safe Withdrawal Rates (SWR), required SIP contributions, and month-by-month investment roadmaps.
    * **The Behavioral Mentor Agent:** Generates empathetic **Hinglish (Hindi+English)** "Nudges" based on Indian spending patterns (e.g., festive spends, 'Pehli Salary').

3.  **Unified Financial Graph (MongoDB Atlas):**
    Our NoSQL database builds an evolving **"User Genome"**—a multi-dimensional profile of income, debt, goals, and risk appetite that grows more accurate with every interaction.

4.  **Regulatory & India Stack Integration:**
    Built to align with the **Account Aggregator (AA)** ecosystem for consented data fetching and **UPI 2.0 Autopay** for frictionless micro-investing.

## The Core Experience: Your 5-Step Path to FIRE

### **1. The Smart Onboarding Quiz (Phase 1)**
A localized, 5-minute interactive experience that assesses your **"Money Health Score"** across 6 dimensions: Emergency preparedness, Insurance, Debt health, Investments, Tax efficiency, and Retirement readiness.

### **2. The Document "Proof" Wizard (Phase 2)**
Users simply upload their Form 16 or paste a bank statement snippet. Our **multimodal AI (Parser)** instantly extracts and verifies the data, turning uncertainty into a verified baseline.

### **3. The Reveal & FIRE Roadmap (Phase 3)**
The AI reveals a comprehensive, month-by-month financial roadmap. We don't just show a target number; we show **"The Corpus"** and the precise SIP amounts needed across diversified asset classes (Index Funds, Gold, FDs).

### **4. Culturally Rooted Hinglish Nudges (Agentic)**
Our mentor identifies behavioral patterns and sends empathetic, **"India-First" Nudges**:
* *Diwali Spend Nudge:* "Bhai, festive season aa raha hai. Thoda budget 'Wants' se 'Savings' mein move kar lein for that Europe trip goal?"
* *Bonus Nudge:* "Arre waah! Bonus credited? Iska 50% Emergency Fund mein daal ke safety complete karte hain?"

### **5. Live Market Intelligence**
The AI-powered advice is **grounded in reality**. Using **yfinance/Alpha Vantage**, Niveshak AI monitors live **Nifty 50** and **Gold price trends** to ensure your goal asset allocations are strategically sound.
